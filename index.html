About the Game

Misty Garden: Blast & Merge mixes simple blasting with really satisfying merging.

Players tap to blast away obstacles and combine matching objects to evolve them into larger structures, gradually clearing away fog to reveal these amazing hidden spots like Moonveil Villa and Coral Castle.

There's always something new to discover, and honestly, it feels pretty rewarding when you finally clear a tough section.

Gameplay Mechanics

The animations are really smooth - there's something satisfying about watching things merge together.

  • Tap to blast through obstacles and clear puzzle elements.

  • Merge identical items to evolve them into more powerful objects.

  • Reveal hidden landmarks and decorative garden elements.

  • Complete stage objectives to unlock new regions of the misty garden.
